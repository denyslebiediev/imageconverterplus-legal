Privacy Policy

Your photos stay yours. Everything is processed on your device — nothing is uploaded, tracked, or shared.

On Your Device

All image conversion, resizing, metadata removal, and watermarking happens entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to a remote server. The App works fully offline.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, crash reports, device identifiers, or any other data. There is no tracking, no telemetry, and no third-party analytics SDK in the App.

Photos & Images

Your photos and images are never uploaded, copied, or transmitted outside your device. The App accesses only the files you explicitly select, processes them locally, and saves the results where you choose.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive, process, or store any payment details, billing information, or Apple ID data.

Share Extension

When you use the Share Extension to send images to the App, those images are processed locally on your device. They are not uploaded or shared with any external service.

Data Retention

We do not store any data on external servers because there are none. All converted files and settings remain on your device and under your control. Deleting the App removes all associated data.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service the App communicates with is Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app purchases. No other external services, APIs, or SDKs are used.

Privacy Manifest

The App includes an Apple-required privacy manifest that declares the App does not collect or track user data. This manifest is reviewed by Apple as part of the App Store review process.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at imageconverterplus.support@gmail.com.
