Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert images?

Tap the + button to select photos from your library or files. Choose your desired output format and quality, then tap Convert. Your converted images will appear in the History tab.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Yes. Select as many images as you'd like and convert them all in a single batch. Each image can have its own format, quality, and size settings.

How do I resize images?

After selecting images, tap on an image to customize its settings. You can set a scale percentage (10-100%) to resize the output.

What does the quality slider do?

The quality slider (0-100%) controls compression for JPEG and HEIC formats. Higher values mean better quality but larger file sizes. PNG and TIFF are lossless and ignore this setting.

Where are my converted images saved?

Converted images are saved within the app's History. From there, you can save them to your Photo Library, share them, or export to Files.

Can I remove metadata from images?

Yes. ImageConverter can strip EXIF data including location, camera info, and timestamps from your converted images for privacy.

What's included in the Pro upgrade?

Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited conversions and all advanced features. There's no subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, restart your device. Still having trouble? Contact us with details about what's happening.
