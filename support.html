Conversion Settings

The quality slider controls compression for JPEG, HEIC, and AVIF formats. Higher values produce better quality at larger file sizes. PNG and TIFF are always lossless and ignore this setting.

Use the size setting to scale images from 50% to 200% of the original dimensions. You can set a default for all images or customize each one individually.

Saving and Sharing

Yes. Go to Settings and choose an auto-delete period — 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or never. Old conversions are removed automatically to save storage.

Troubleshooting

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, restart your device and make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store. Still having trouble? Contact us with details about what's happening and we'll help.

Make sure your device has enough free storage. Very large images may require more available space. Try converting fewer images at once, or reduce the output size. If the problem continues, contact us with the image format and size.
